The Savanna

A savanna or savannah is a mixed woodland-grassland (i.e. grassy woodland) ecosystem characterised by the
trees being sufficiently widely spaced so that the canopy does not close.

white and black stripes. The zebra are (source) also frequent travelers.

Savanna animal two, giraffes. Giraffes are up next in the savanna wildlife. They are animals with large necks and legs
