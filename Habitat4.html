The Mangrove

"A mangrove is a shrub or tree that grows mainly in coastal saline or brackish water. Mangroves grow in an equatorial
climate, typically along coastlines and tidal rivers. They have special adaptations to take in extra oxygen and to
remove salt, which allow them to tolerate conditions that would kill most plants."

The mangroves have first originated in Late Cretaceous to Paleocene epochs, and were spread apart extensively due

to the Plate motion, or the movement of techonomic plates. Further, the oldest found fossils of the Mangrove go as far as

75 million years ago. But what are mangroves? The trees that make up these mangroves contain a "complex" salt filtration

system as well as A root system that is useful to the mangrove trees, to help with the saltwater immersion and wave action.

The Proboscis Monkey


The Proboscis Monkey is one of the largest species, and being one of largest in Asia. They can be also found with a large

coat of fur which can come in colors of reddish brown, yellowish brown, or bright orange. One of the defining features of

this monkey is the large nose it has, which may surpass 10.2 cm in length or 4 inches. The nose also has specialized skulls

with a specific nasal cartilages that are used to support the large nose they have. Their diets manly consist of

fruits and leaves.


he Salt Water Crocodile


The saltwater crocodile (Crocodylus porosus) is a crocodilian native to saltwater habitats, brackish wetlands
and freshwater rivers from India's east coast across Southeast Asia and the Sundaic region to northern Australia and Micronesia.

Salt water crocodiles can be known as the largest living reptile as males and females can reach lengths of 6m or 20ft and

3m of 10ft respectfully. They also have a wider snout compared to other crocodiles with the length of the crocodiles snout

being twice that of the snouts width. They also have small or no scales at all, which can be used to determine if the crocodile

is a saltwater crocodile which may come in handy. Lastly, the diet of these salt water crocodiles consist of what is available

at the moment as they are not concerned with what they eat and can go without eating food for a long while.
